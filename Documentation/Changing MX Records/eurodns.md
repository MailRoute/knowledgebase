## MX Record Configuration: eurodns

  1. Log in to your account at eurodns.com.
  2. Click **My Domains** in the **My Domains** menu on the left side of the page. A table listing your domains should appear.
  3. Click the **DNS** column on the row of the domain you wish to change. The DNS records for your domain should now be displayed.
  4. If you have one or more records in the **MAIL EXCHANGER/SERVER** table (these are your existing MX records), click on the **Delete** link relative to that record.
  5. When all the MX records have been deleted, from the **Add... **drop-down menu, choose **New Mail Server** and click the **Add** button next to it.
  6. In the **Priority :** field, add the priority: **10**.
  7. In the **Mail Server :** field add the mail server name: **mail.mailroute.net**
  8. Click the **Save Settings** button.
