## MX Record Configuration: EveryDNS.net

  1. Log in to your account at EveryDNS.net.
  2. On the left side, click the domain you'd like to use with MailRoute.
  3. Since EveryDNS.net is your hosting service, and not your domain registrar, be sure that your domain points to EveryDNS.net's nameservers. This will allow your MX record configuration to take effect.
  4. Below **Add a Record:**, enter the following: 
    * Fully Qualified Domain Name: (enter your domain)
    * Record Type: **MX**
    * Record Value: **mail.mailroute.net. **(be sure to include the trailing dot "."!)
    * If MX Record, MX Value: **1**
  5. Click **Submit**.
