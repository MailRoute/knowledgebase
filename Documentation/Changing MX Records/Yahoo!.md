## MX Record Configuration: Yahoo!

  1. Log in to the administrative console for your domain.    
        To log in, go to http://smallbusiness.yahoo.com, click the **Sign in to Small Business** button, and enter the Yahoo ID and password for your administration account.  
  2. Select **Domain Control Panel** located at the left side of the domain you are updating. 
  3. Click **Manage Advanced DNS Settings**
  4. Scroll halfway down the page and click **Change MX Records**.
  5. Under **MailServer Hostname** be sure to make note of any existing MX records in case you need to restore them later.
  6. In the first open text box, change the hostname to **mail.mailroute.net** and set the priority to **10**.
  7. Delete the old MX records.
  8. Click **Submit**.
