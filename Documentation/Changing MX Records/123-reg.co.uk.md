  1. Log in to your account at 123-reg.co.uk.
  2. Choose the domain you wish to use with MailRoute in the **Manage Domains** drop-down box, and click **Modify Domain**.
  3. Choose the fifth option - **Manage DNS**.  
        Two default MX records will be visible: 
        * mx0.123-reg.co.uk. 10
        * mx1.123-reg.co.uk. 20
  4. Replace the first entry with: **mail.mailroute.net. 1 ** (don't forget the trailing dot "." after mail.mailroute.net!)
  5. Click **Update DNS**
  6. Delete the second entry.
  7. Click *Update DNS*
