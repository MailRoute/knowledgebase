A **Customer** may have several types of **Contacts**

* Billing
* Technical
* Emergency

A *Billing* contact will receive invoices from MailRoute or their associated Reseller.

A *Technical* contact receives emails and notices about the **MailRoute Email Protection Service**

An *Emergency* contact will be used if we need to contact you in case of an emergency, such as when we detect that your email server is down and not accepting email.

One person can belong to more than one of these categories.

We recommend that you have one of each type of **Contact** for your **Customer** account.  

Click the **Contact** tab in the **Customer** detail page to view your contacts.  You can edit the contact by clicking on their name, or change the type of contact by clicking in the appropriate boxes.  The red **X** will delete the **Contact** of course.

![Customer Contact List](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/contact_list.png)

Click the **Add Contact** button to add a contact.
