You can also put email delivery for a domain on *hold*.  We will accept email for a domain that's on hold, but we won't try to deliver it to your mailserver.  We'll queue it up until you release the hold.  

This is useful if you want to pause delivery while you upgrade a mailserver, you're making changes to your user lists, etc.

You can put email delivery on *hold* two different ways.

1.  Click in the *Delivery Status* column of a **Domain** list.

    ![Domain List](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/domain_list.png)

2.  Click on the *Delivery & MailServers* tab when viewing a domain,  then on the *Hold Delivery* tab.

    ![Delivery Hold](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/delivery_hold.png)
