The **Email Account** list provides access to each of the **Email Accounts** for which you are an **Administrator**.  

Simply click on an **Email Accounts** to edit the **Email Accounts** or to manage their settings, white/blacklists and quarantine.  Click the red **X** at the far right of the **Email Account** to delete it.  

**Important** Deleting a **Email Account** will delete the **Email Account** and all of it's associated whitelists, blacklists and settings..  There is no way to undo this!

![Email Account List](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/emailaccount_list.png)

There are a couple ways you can add email accounts.

1.  Add an **Email Account** by clicking on the **Add User** button.  
    
    ![Email Account Add](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/emailaccount_add.png)

2.  Select "Bulk Add Email Accounts..." from the "More Actions" menu. Enter the localparts of the Email Accounts into the dialog box.  This is the part to the left of the "@" symbol in the email address.
    
    ![Email Account Bulk Add](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/emailaccount_add_bulk.png)

3.  Import email accounts from a text file.  Select "Import..." from the "More Actions" menu.
    
    ![Email Account Import](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/emailaccount_import.png)


## Reassign as alias

If you've entered a bunch of email accounts, and you decide you want one or more of them to instead be an alias of another account, you can reassign them as aliases.

Select the **Email Accounts**, and then choose "Reassign as alias..." from the "More Actions" menu.  Start typing the email address to which you wish to assign it, and then choose it from the auto-filling pulldown menu that appears.  Click OK and the **Email Address** will be made into an alias.
