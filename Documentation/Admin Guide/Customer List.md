The **Customer** list provides access to each of the Customers for which you are an **Administrator**.  

Simply click on a **Customer** to edit the **Customer** or to manage their **Domains**.  Click the red **X** at the far right of the **Customer** to delete it.  

**Important** Deleting a **Customer** will delete the **Customer** and all of it's associated **Domains** and **Email Accounts**.  There is no way to undo this!

![Customer List](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/customer_list.png)

Add a **Customer** by clicking on the **Add Customer** button.  

![Customer List](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/customer_add.png)
