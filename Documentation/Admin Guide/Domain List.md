The **Domain** list provides access to each of the Domains for which you are an **Administrator**.  

Simply click on a **Domain** to edit the **Domain** or to manage their **Email Accounts**.  Click the red **X** at the far right of the **Domain** to delete it.  

**Important** Deleting a **Domain** will delete the **Domain** and all of it's associated settings and **Email Accounts**.  There is no way to undo this!

You can *deactivate* a domain by clicking in the "Active" column or on the green dot to the left of the domain name.  We will not accept email sent to *inactive* domains - it will be bounced.

You can also put email delivery for a domain on *hold* by clicking in the "Hold" column.  We will accept email for a domain that's on hold, but we won't try to deliver it to your mailserver.  We'll queue it up until you release the hold.  This is useful if you want to pause delivery while you upgrade a mailserver, for example.

The *Domain List* displays both **Domains** and **Domain Aliases** for quick reference.  The *type* column indicates whether the domain is a parent domain or an alias.
