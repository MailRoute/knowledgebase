## Quarantine Notifications

We can send notifications of messages in the quarantine right to your inbox of your users.  

**Important:** Per-user quarantine notifications are only available if you have selected the "Email Account list is complete" option on the ![Email Account List]().

A quarantine notification looks like this:

![Notification - Sample](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/notif_sample.png)

You can choose which nights of the week you wish these messages to be sent for all the users in your domain.  They have the option of changing this in their own settings.

![Notification - Set](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/notif_set.png)
