## The Quarantine

The Quarantine is where we store email that has been filtered out from your account.  These are emails that are spam, virus infected, have bad headers and the like.  They are stored for a minimum of 15 days.

Click the tabs to view the different types of quarantined emails:

![MailRoute Quarantine](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/qtn.png)

You can select multiple messages for deleting, whitelisting or recovering to your inbox (or both) by selecting their checkboxes and clicking the appropriate button.  

Click on the column headers to sort by that column.

You may view an individual message by clicking on it's Subject.

![MailRoute Quarantine](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/qtn_msg.png)
