### Changing your password from within the Control Panel

If you're logged in and want to change your password, just click the **settings** link at the top right of the Control Panel:

![MailRoute Control Panel - Settings](https://static.mailroute.net/kb/settings.jpg "MailRoute Control Panel - Settings")


And then change your password:

![MailRoute Control Panel - Password](https://static.mailroute.net/kb/password.jpg "MailRoute Control Panel - Password")
