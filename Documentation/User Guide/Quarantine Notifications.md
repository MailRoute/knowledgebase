## Quarantine Notifications

We can send notifications of messages in the quarantine right to your inbox.  If your domain administrator has provided us with a complete list of users for your domain, then this feature is available to you.

A quarantine notification looks like this:

![Notification - Sample](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/notif_sample.png)

You can choose which nights of the week you wish these messages to be sent, or go with the default setting your administrator has chosen:

![Notification - Set](http://static.mailroute.net/kb/notif_set.png)
