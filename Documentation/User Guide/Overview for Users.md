
MailRoute is an email filtering service.  It's a __cloud__ service - one that exists on and runs over the internet.  The nuts and bolts of how it all works are transparent to you.  As an end-user, you can simply sit back and enjoy the safe and secure spam and virus free email environment that your email system administrators have created for you.

The MailRoute service is designed to be **set and forget**.  Most users will find that the standard settings suit them perfectly, but if you have specific needs or want to tweak the settings you'll find that you can customize the filtering to your heart's content.

### The MailRoute Control Panel

You can find the MailRoute Control Panel at [https://admin.mailroute.net](https://admin.mailroute.net).  

Login with your email address and the password that your administrator provided, and you can view your quarantined email and adjust your settings.
