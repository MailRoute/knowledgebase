###ActiveDirectory and LDAP Synchronization by MailRoute

###### What is this feature? 
MailRoute has added automatic synchronization for ActiveDirectory and LDAP servers for easy account management. In short, our servers talk to your servers to get a list of your users. Any changes you make to your user lists will be automatically updated in your MailRoute account.

MailRoute will then recognize mail for those users, and we'll use this list to provide an accurate user count for billing purposes.

###### Why do I need it? 

Your life just got easier! If you haven't been using MailRoute's API to manage your user lists, then with ActiveDirectory and LDAP sync, you no longer have to add or delete users manually in your MailRoute account.

###### How do I use it?
You can setup your servers to sync manually or automatically. Even if you choose automatically scheduled syncs, MailRoute recommends doing manual tests of this feature periodically. Or, you can simply do manual syncs whenever you like.
