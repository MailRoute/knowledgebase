## MX Record Configuration: Netfirms

  1. Log in to the [Members Control Panel](https://secure.netfirms.com/secure/login.bml).
  2. Click **Domains**.
  3. For the desired domain, click **Admin**.
  4. Click **MX Record**.
  5. Add the following entries:
    * **mail.mailroute.net**
