## MX Record Configuration: Lxlabs

  1. Log in to your domain at [LXLabs](http://lx-labs.com/).
  2. Delete all MX records for your domain.
  3. Click on **Manage DNS** \--> **MX records**
  4. Enter **_mail.mailroute.net_** at priority **10**.
